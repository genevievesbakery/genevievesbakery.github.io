About

Welcome to La Boulangerie de Genevieve, where every loaf tells a story and every pastry brings joy to your palate. Established in 2023 by Laurie, our bakery is a homage to her daughter, Genevieve, whose love for baking inspired this quaint haven of culinary delights nestled in the heart of Shreveport, Louisiana.

At La Boulangerie de Genevieve, we believe in the artistry of traditional French baking techniques coupled with the warmth of Southern hospitality. Our journey began with a simple desire to share our passion for exquisite pastries and artisanal bread with the community.
